About

The Nigerian Gaming Community (NGC) is a platform that was
created for Nigerian gamers to interact with fellow Nigerian gamers and
compete along side or against one another for the prize to be tagged the best in the country.

Why Was The NGC Created?

from the CEO, Bluestorm912

I have searched the internet, social media and i have seen over a hundred organization, group of people, individual who create gaming competition.
Now one might ask isn't that a good thing?.

Well, let me break it down for you. lets say out of those 100 organiations, only 10 are the ones that organize pubg competitons.
Mr.A is a great pubg player but due to lack of communication he's aware of just 5 pubg competition created, but unfortunately he can only compete in 3 of them and that is because some competions were held same day so he would have to decide. As a great pubg player he wins all the 3 competitions , Mr. A will only be knnown by approx. 230 players instead of the 300 player (3 competions, 100 people each) due to the fact that 1 person can also go for the same 3 competitions.
Now he's potential to be a great pubg player in nigeria is limited. This is what the NGC is trying to fix.
The NGC is not an organizer but it's a body that those organiations can create game competitons under and the competitions will be managed properly in a manner that no 2 competitions will clash with one another and also when competitions are created under the NGC or in co-operation with the NGC the winner, most likey Mr. A will now recieve a national recongnition and as well as global reconfnition.
